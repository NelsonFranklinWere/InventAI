About Us

The Hidden Gem Behind StriveGo

Our Story

StriveGo was founded by four ambitious Kenyan IT Professionals who saw the potential of AI to transform businesses in Kenya and beyond. We're leveraging our Skills to build tools that automate daily activities and create jobs.

Our mission is to automate manual tasks creating opportunities for other young Kenyans and Ensure Productivity in Businesses. We believe in making Kenya the beast of AI by developing accessible and scalable solutions for day-to-day problems in e-commerce, business, tech, Apps, and websites.

We're not just building a company; we're sparking a revival of young Kenyan entrepreneurs who use technology to solve real problems and create Chances of Survival to our community.

Our Vision

What We Speak

To design and deliver AI-powered solutions that automate business operations, empower SMEs, and open new opportunities for young entrepreneurs in Kenya.

We focus on building practical, affordable, and scalable tech products from WhatsApp bots to websites, mobile apps, and edutech platforms that drive growth and create real impact.

At StriveGo, our mission goes beyond technology it's about empowering people. Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to set up a WhatsApp bot?

The setup time depends on the complexity of your requirements. Simple bots can be set up in 1-2 weeks, while more complex solutions with custom integrations may take 3-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you offer training on how to use the systems?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for all our solutions. This includes documentation, video tutorials, and live training sessions to ensure you and your team can effectively use and manage the systems we implement.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time as your business grows. We'll help migrate your data and settings seamlessly to the new plan without any downtime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa payments, bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. We can also invoice businesses for monthly or annual subscriptions.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer different levels of support depending on your plan. This includes email support, priority support, and 24/7 dedicated support for enterprise clients. We're committed to ensuring your systems run smoothly.
